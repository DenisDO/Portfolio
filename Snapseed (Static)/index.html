 KEY FEATURES 
Snapseed users can edit pictures using swiping gestures to select different effects and enhancements. Users can directly share the images on social networking sites like Facebook and Instagram.
FILTERS
29 tools and filters, including Spot Correction, Brush, Structure, HDR Effect, Perspective.
YOUR STYLES
Allows you to save your styles and apply them to new photos. Brush selective correction.
CROP
Crop: Allows you to crop a picture in a standard format or arbitrarily.
RAW PROCESSING
RAW processing: allows to work with RAW-files of type DNG, save them without loss of quality or export to JPG.
CORRECTION
Correction: allows you to adjust the exposure and color automatically or manually with high accuracy.
GRUNGE
Grunge: gives the picture an original, bold character due to color styles and texture mapping (5 basic styles and endless variations).
